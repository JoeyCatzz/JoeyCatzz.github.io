What Warren Buffet is looking for in an income statement

  1. Net earnings show great consistency (upward trend) over the last 10 years.
  2. Gross Profit margin > 40% (gross profit margin = gross profit /revenue *100)
  3. Net margin > 20% (net margin = net earnings/revenue *100)

What Warren Buffet is looking for in a balancesheet

  1. Retained earnings show great consistency over the last 10 years.
  2. Return on equity (ROE) net earnings/total shareholders equity * 100)
  3. Little to no long term debt: long term debt is smaller then net earnings /4
